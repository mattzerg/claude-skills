blocks: ["Customer segments", "Value propositions", "Channels", "Customer relationships", "Revenue streams", "Key resources", "Key activities", "Key partnerships", "Cost structure"]
when_to_use:
  - Designing or stress-testing a new business model (especially early-stage)
  - Comparing two business-model alternatives on the same page
  - Onboarding a team to the full economics + GTM + ops of a venture
when_not_to_use:
  - Mature business where the canvas would just describe history — pick a sharper question
  - Single-decision contexts (use 2x2 or weighted-scoring)
anti_patterns:
  - Filling all 9 blocks with adjectives instead of named entities
  - Key partnerships used as wishful thinking — list contracts, not aspirations
  - Cost structure with no named largest line item
