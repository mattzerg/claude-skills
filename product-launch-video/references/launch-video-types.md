# Launch Video Types

## Hero Launch Film

Use for a company, flagship product, or major product line launch. Needs a strong thesis, memorable visual identity, product proof, and a clear CTA. Usually 45-90 seconds.

## Launch Video System

Use when the launch needs one deeper anchor video plus multiple shorter videos. This is often the right architecture for a serious product launch:

- **Anchor video**: one 75-100 second video that carries the full story, category context, product proof, trust, and CTA.
- **Modular clips**: several 20-30 second videos, each built around one hook, audience, use case, proof moment, or channel.

Do not treat the short videos as simple cutdowns of the long video. Some can be cutdowns, but the strongest ones usually have their own first frame, hook, proof order, and CTA.

Use the anchor video for homepage, launch blog, YouTube, sales follow-up, founder post, and investor/customer context. Use modular clips for LinkedIn, X/Twitter, paid, Product Hunt, sales snippets, retargeting, and follow-up posts.

## Feature Launch Demo

Use when one capability is the news. Show before/after, the feature in action, and one or two adjacent outcomes. Usually 20-60 seconds.

## Social Teaser

Use before or during launch week. One idea, one visual transformation, one CTA. Usually 6-20 seconds.

## Modular Launch Clip

Use as one piece in a launch video system. Usually 20-30 seconds. Each clip should have one job:

- One audience segment
- One pain point
- One product proof moment
- One use case
- One objection answered
- One customer or benchmark proof point
- One CTA

## Founder Announcement

Use when trust, timing, or narrative matters. Pair direct-to-camera conviction with product proof and b-roll. Usually 30-120 seconds.

## Product Hunt / Community Launch

Use for fast scanning. Lead with what it does, show the product, include CTA and URL/product name. Often needs square and vertical variants.

## Sales / Customer Launch Clip

Use when the launch supports pipeline. Focus on buyer pain, proof, use case, and next meeting/demo CTA.

## Homepage Loop

Use as silent ambient proof. Avoid dense narration dependency. Make the product state readable and loop cleanly.
