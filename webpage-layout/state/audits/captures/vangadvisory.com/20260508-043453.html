How we work

Engagements run 4–12 weeks of focused work, with optional retainer for ongoing operating support. Founder-led directly by Matt or a senior partner, never offshored, occasionally with a curated bench of 49+ specialists across design, content, data engineering, paid media, and product.

Day rates: $2,000 for startups, $2,500 for blockchains and funds, $3,000 for corporates. Discounts for scopes of 10+ days. Time/retainer or task/deliverable structures both work. Open to performance alignment, advisory equity, or partial cash + equity for early-stage clients — several of those have become Vang Capital portfolio companies.
