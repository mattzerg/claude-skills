# Production Modes

## Screen Recording

Capture clean product states, seeded accounts, realistic data, cursor paths, loading states, before/after states, and error-free takes. Record at high resolution and crop later. Keep raw recordings, marked selects, and notes about where each action appears.

## Live Action

Plan talent, location, lighting, audio, wardrobe, props, releases, and pickup lines. Prioritize audio quality over camera novelty.

## Remote Interview

Prepare question flow, pre-interview notes, consent, recording settings, backup audio, b-roll needs, and transcript workflow.

## Motion Graphics

Track source assets, brand rules, animation references, type treatments, logo usage, and export specs. Confirm whether the video needs editable project files.

## Mixed Media

Create a source map so editors know what is filmed, recorded, designed, generated, licensed, or still missing.

## Event / Webinar

Plan capture windows, speaker permissions, slide sources, backup recording, highlight timestamps, and post-event clipping format.
