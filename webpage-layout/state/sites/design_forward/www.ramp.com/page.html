Ramp enforces policy in real time. 3. **Capture:** Receipts auto-captured at swipe or submitted via SMS/Slack; invoices extracted by OCR on upload. 4. **AI processing:** Policy Agent reviews expenses; AP agents auto-code, detect fraud, route approvals, schedule payment. 5. **Matching:** Invoices matched to POs and receipts; discrepancies flagged, payment blocked until resolved. 6. **Payment:** Validated spend processed via ACH, card, check, or wire — zero fees on domestic ACH and checks. 7. **Sync:** Transactions auto-coded and synced to ERP (NetSuite, QBO, Xero, Sage, Workday, Oracle). 8. **Intelligence:** Real-time dashboards surface duplicate subscriptions, overspend, unused licenses, and savings opportunities. --- ## Implementation checklist (for admins) - Choose pricing tier (Free, Plus, or Enterprise) based on integration and automation needs. - Connect ERP (QuickBooks, Xero, NetSuite, Sage Intacct, Workday, or Oracle);
