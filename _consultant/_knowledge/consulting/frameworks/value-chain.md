name: Porter Value Chain
primary_activities: ["Inbound logistics", "Operations", "Outbound logistics", "Marketing & sales", "Service"]
support_activities: ["Firm infrastructure", "HR management", "Technology development", "Procurement"]
when_to_use:
  - Identifying where margin is captured vs lost across a company's activities
  - Considering vertical integration (which stages to own vs buy)
  - Reorg / outsourcing decisions
when_not_to_use:
  - Pure software / SaaS with no physical logistics (the primary activities don't map cleanly — use a software-specific value-stream map)
  - Network businesses (value is in match quality, not stages)
anti_patterns:
  - Treating support activities as overhead — they often carry differentiation
  - Mapping activities without margin/cost data per stage
chart_recipe: bar
---

Each stage gets a margin number + a strategic-importance score. Lopsided = a fixable issue or a hidden moat.
