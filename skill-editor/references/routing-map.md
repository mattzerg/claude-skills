# Skill Routing Map

Use this map when multiple skills could plausibly trigger. Prefer the narrowest leaf skill unless the user asks for orchestration or a final gate.

## Review And Ship

- `review-pack`: choose a multi-skill review stack and order the passes.
- `ship-gate`: make the final go/no-go readiness call after required evidence is known.
- `fakematt-feedback`: broad product, UX, IA, and usability critique of URLs, Figma, local pages, or static mockups.
- `cro-auditor`: marketing/signup funnel conversion audit, proof gaps, CTA hierarchy, measurement blockers, and experiments.
- `graphic-layout`: static rendered asset composition, balance, whitespace, cropping, hierarchy, and text fit.
- `brand-check`: brand-system correctness: logo, palette, typography, hierarchy, and brand-guide compliance.
- `swag-design`: physical merchandise, vendor specs, item anatomy, production artwork, and multi-item review PDFs.
- `video-review`: product/demo videos, motion exports, pacing, hook timing, audio ending, jitter, and export checks.

## Brand And Assets

- `brand-kit-creator`: collect, normalize, and package source brand assets into a usable kit.
- `brand-guide-creator`: create or update brand rules from assets, sites, and collateral.
- `brand-check`: audit finished visual assets against the brand system.
- `graphic-layout`: audit finished visual assets for composition independent of brand correctness.

## Matt Voice

- `fakematt-email`: professional external email.
- `fakematt-personal`: family and close personal email.
- `fakematt-copyedit`: prose drafts, thought pieces, posts, launch writing, and external copy.
- `fakematt-launch`: launch packages in Matt's voice.
- `fakematt-feedback`: product/UX review in Matt's review style, not prose copyediting.

## Launch

- `launch-announcement`: review or scaffold the announcement post.
- `fakematt-launch`: draft launch packages and channel copy.
- `launch-ops`: turn the launch into an executable rollout plan.
- `utm-attribution`: create or validate external Zerg links before posting.
- `ship-gate`: final readiness decision before publish/send/launch.

## Planning And Specs

- `ui-designer`: product interface structure, flows, states, component choices, and handoff notes.
- `dashboard-spec`: measurement design, KPIs, event definitions, chart lineup, freshness, and decision thresholds.
- `experiment-designer`: hypothesis, variants, metrics, guardrails, sample target, and scale/kill/rerun rules.
- `process-streamliner`: recurring workflow redesign, SOPs, ownership, automation opportunities, and handoff rules.
- `cro-auditor`: conversion surface diagnosis and experiment opportunities before formal test design.
- `launch-ops`: one-time launch execution planning, blockers, owners, source-of-truth artifacts, and day-of sequence.
- `ship-gate`: final readiness verdict once reviews/specs/plans exist.

## Connectors

- `gmail-skill`, `gcal-skill`, `slack-skill`, `github-skill`, `linear-skill`, and `zergboard-skill` own direct app/work-system actions.
- Use a connector skill when the user asks to read, search, send, create, update, or manage live data in that system.
- Use non-connector skills first when the user is only drafting, auditing, planning, or reviewing.
