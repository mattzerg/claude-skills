# Item Zones And Patterns

## Apparel

- T-shirt front: hero graphic centered chest, full logo above small, sleeve, back neck, or hem tag.
- T-shirt back: full logo at yoke or large back print only if campaign graphic is not already dominant.
- Hoodie: left chest embroidery, sleeve hit, cuff, back yoke, woven hem tag. Avoid detailed embroidery over pouch pocket or zipper.
- Zip hoodie: never center a graphic across the zipper unless intentionally split.
- Sweatshirt: chest graphic can be larger than hoodie; logo can go on sleeve or back neck.

## Headwear

- Cap front: compact mark or short wordmark only.
- Cap side/back: full wordmark, small phrase, or woven tab.
- Under-brim: hidden campaign line or pattern.
- Avoid long command strings on curved front panels unless reduced to a patch.

## Drinkware

- Mug hero zone: side opposite handle.
- Secondary logo: handle side, under panel, inner rim, or bottom mark.
- Wrap art: keep safe area away from handle by at least 0.5 in unless vendor template says otherwise.
- Bottles: vertical logo/wordmark works better than horizontal text on narrow cylinders.

## Stickers And Patches

- Sticker hero: one strong graphic, thick enough border for die-cut tolerance.
- Sticker sheet: full logo in sheet header or backing card, not necessarily on every tiny sticker if the sheet is the item.
- Patch: use fewer colors, thicker strokes, fewer words.
- Embroidery: avoid type below roughly 0.2 in cap height without vendor confirmation.

## Desk Goods

- Notebook front: hero mark or campaign graphic centered or lower-right; full logo on spine, back cover, belly band, or label.
- Keycard: full logo top-left or issuer area; campaign graphic lower half or back.
- Lanyard: repeat full logo and/or compact mark, spaced evenly; avoid long phrases unless large woven strap.

## Review Layout

- First page: contact-sheet overview of all physical items.
- Next pages: one item per page, showing item silhouette, logo zone callouts, and production notes.
- Final pages: flat production artwork and vendor spec.
