# Channel Versioning

Use this before scripting or production when the video will ship in multiple places. Adapt the idea per channel instead of only cropping the master.

For bigger launches, plan two layers:

- **Anchor**: one 75-100 second canonical video for the full story.
- **Clips**: multiple 20-30 second modular videos for channel-specific distribution.

## Channel Rules

| Channel | Best Runtime | First Frame | CTA | Notes |
|---|---:|---|---|---|
| Homepage hero | 10-45s loop or 45-90s explainer | Product state or transformation | Try, book, learn more | Must work muted; avoid tiny UI. |
| Launch blog embed | 30-90s | Title + product proof | Read / try / share | Can carry more context than social. |
| X / Twitter | 6-30s | Pattern interrupt or visible result | Try / watch / reply | Needs strong captions and mobile crop. |
| LinkedIn | 15-60s | Human or business outcome | Learn / book / comment | Concrete professional value beats vague hype. |
| Product Hunt | 15-45s | What it does, immediately | Upvote / try | Fast scanning; product name and URL visible. |
| YouTube | 60-180s | Clear topic and payoff | Subscribe / try / watch more | Can support fuller explanation. |
| Paid social | 6-30s | Problem or outcome | Sign up / book / install | Make multiple hooks. |
| Sales email | 20-90s | Buyer pain or customer proof | Book / reply / watch demo | Needs buyer-specific proof. |
| Internal launch | 60-180s | Why it matters to team | Align / enable / share | Include context and rollout details. |

## Anchor Plus Clips

| Asset | Runtime | Best Channels | Job | Notes |
|---|---:|---|---|---|
| Anchor video | 75-100s | Homepage, blog, YouTube, sales follow-up | Full narrative and proof | Can be chaptered into clips but should stand alone. |
| Social proof clip | 20-30s | LinkedIn, X, paid | Show one proof moment | Needs own hook and first frame. |
| Use-case clip | 20-30s | LinkedIn, sales email, retargeting | Speak to one audience/workflow | Change opening line per segment. |
| Founder clip | 20-30s | LinkedIn, X, launch post | Add human conviction | Product proof should still appear quickly. |
| Product Hunt clip | 15-30s | Product Hunt, community | Explain what it does fast | Product name and URL visible. |

## Overlay And Crop Checks

- Keep important text and UI clear of bottom captions and platform controls.
- Make 9:16 compositions intentionally vertical; do not rely on center-cropping dense desktop UI.
- Create a first-frame or thumbnail direction for every distribution channel.
- Decide whether captions are burned-in, sidecar files, or platform-native.
