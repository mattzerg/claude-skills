# Measurement Plan

Use this when the launch video needs success criteria, variant testing, reporting, or a connection to launch goals. For formal experiments use `$experiment-designer`; for dashboards use `$dashboard-spec`; for tracked links use `$utm-attribution`.

## Match Metrics To Job

| Video Job | Primary Signal | Supporting Signals |
|---|---|---|
| Awareness | 3-second hold or thumb-stop rate | Reach, shares, comments, completion |
| Education | Completion rate | Replays, site depth, demo clicks |
| Conversion | CTA click or signup | Landing conversion, cost per action |
| Sales enablement | Reply or booked meeting | Watch rate, forwarded/shared signals |
| Launch proof | Product page engagement | Waitlist joins, trial starts, announcement reads |
| Internal enablement | Team comprehension/action | Enablement completion, asset usage |

## Basic Measurement Spec

- Goal
- Audience
- Channel
- Primary metric
- Guardrail metric
- Tracking link or event
- Reporting window
- Decision rule: keep, cut down, reshoot, make variants, or scale distribution

## Variant Testing

Test only one major variable at a time:

- Hook
- First frame / thumbnail
- Runtime
- CTA
- Proof order
- Caption style
- Founder-led vs product-led

## Practical Defaults

- Social: compare first 3 seconds, completion, and profile/site click.
- Homepage: compare play engagement, scroll depth, CTA click, and signup/demo conversion.
- Sales email: compare thumbnail click, reply rate, and meeting conversion.
- Paid: compare thumb-stop, CTR, CPA, and landing conversion.
