# Launch Brief Intake

Use this when the user gives a rough or incomplete launch-video request. Do not block on every missing field; infer conservative defaults and mark hard unknowns.

## Required Fields

- Product or feature name
- Launch moment: waitlist, beta, public launch, feature release, funding/news, version release, community launch, sales enablement, homepage refresh
- Audience
- Current behavior or pain
- New behavior enabled by the product
- Proof available: UI, workflow, customer quote, metric, benchmark, technical detail, founder POV, social proof
- Channel: homepage, X/Twitter, LinkedIn, Product Hunt, YouTube, sales email, conference, paid ad, in-product, internal
- Runtime target
- CTA

## Useful Fields

- Brand tone
- Competitors or category expectations
- Screens or flows that can be shown
- Claims requiring approval
- Customer names or logos allowed
- Legal/security constraints
- Deadline
- Production mode: screen recording, motion graphics, live action, founder, mixed media
- Needed aspect ratios

## Conservative Defaults

- Runtime: 45 seconds for a broad launch package, 20 seconds for social, 60 seconds for homepage or founder-led launch.
- Production mode: screen recording plus motion graphics for software products.
- CTA: "Try it" or "Join the waitlist" when launch stage is unclear.
- Variants: 16:9 master plus 9:16 social cutdown when distribution is unclear.

## Hard Unknowns To Surface

- Missing product proof
- Missing CTA
- Claims that sound measurable but lack sources
- Unclear audience
- Unclear product availability or launch date
- Unavailable UI or unreleased product states
