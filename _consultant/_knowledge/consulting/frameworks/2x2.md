---
id: 2x2
name: 2x2 Matrix
axes: [x, y]
default_axes: {x: "Effort", y: "Impact"}
quadrants: ["High impact / Low effort (DO NOW)", "High impact / High effort (PLAN)", "Low impact / Low effort (BACKLOG)", "Low impact / High effort (KILL)"]
when_to_use:
  - Categorizing options across two independent dimensions where action differs by quadrant
  - Forcing a portfolio call (which items to prioritize, which to drop)
  - When the dimensions are genuinely orthogonal (effort vs impact, urgency vs importance, defensibility vs growth)
when_not_to_use:
  - Items don't naturally split (all cluster in one quadrant — axes are wrong)
  - More than two important dimensions matter (use weighted-scoring or Pugh instead)
  - The two axes are correlated (effort and complexity, for example)
anti_patterns:
  - Picking axes that confirm a preordained answer
  - Quadrant labels that aren't actions ("Stars" / "Dogs" without a verb)
  - Same item plotted in two quadrants — discrete classification only
chart_recipe: scatter-2x2
---

A 2x2 must change the recommendation. If the quadrants look like 3-1 or all-in-one, the axes are wrong.
